Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2024

1. Introduction

Alok Library ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our student subscription management application and services.

By using our application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information from students:

  • Name: Full name for identification and account management
  • Phone Number: Contact information for communication
  • Address: Physical address for record-keeping
  • Aadhar Card Number: Government-issued identification number (12 digits) for verification
  • Profile Picture: Optional profile image (auto-generated if not provided)

2.2 Subscription Information

  • Subscription start and expiry dates
  • Subscription duration (in months)
  • Payment amount and status
  • Payment history

2.3 Account Information

  • Username and encrypted password
  • User role (student, teacher, or admin)
  • Account creation and last login timestamps

2.4 Device Information

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens for push notifications
  • Device information (if collected by mobile app)

2.5 Usage Data

  • Notes created by students
  • Notification preferences
  • Application usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your student account
  • Subscription Management: To track and manage your library subscription status
  • Communication: To send you notifications about exams, events, library updates, and important announcements
  • Payment Processing: To record and track subscription payments
  • Service Delivery: To provide access to library services and resources
  • Analytics: To improve our services and understand usage patterns (aggregated, anonymized data)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Authorized Personnel: Admin and teacher accounts can view student information for management purposes
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating our application (e.g., cloud hosting, database services)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests
  • Safety and Security: We may share information to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing
  • Data transmission is secured using HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Database access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications to your mobile device. To provide this service:

  • We collect and store FCM tokens associated with your account
  • Tokens are used solely for sending notifications about library updates, exams, events, and important announcements
  • You can opt-out of push notifications by uninstalling the app or removing your FCM token
  • FCM tokens are stored securely and are not shared with third parties except Firebase (Google) for notification delivery

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

When you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for students of all ages. We take special care to protect the privacy of minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have that information removed.

10. Third-Party Services

Our application may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): For push notifications and analytics. See Google Privacy Policy
  • Hosting Services: For application hosting and database services

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you a notification through the application (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted on this page.

12. International Data Transfers
